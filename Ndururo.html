Welcome for Bible training

Ndururo Bible School is a chartered institution with both inservice and reqular students ,located in Ndururo, Kapsabet Road,
approximately 6.5 kilometres (4.0 mi), by road, northwest of the central business district of eldoret town, in central kenya.
‘IN GOD WE EXCEL’, Ndururo School has produced nternational Bible ministers who have benefited the country and more important abroad worldwide for the services they offer.
Ndururo Bible college was founded by american missionary, dr. JOHANA KIPLETING , a retired missionary director [general conference], in 1976.
